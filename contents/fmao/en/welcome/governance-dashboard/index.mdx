*From Many, as One's* on-chain governance system powered by RAKSHA guardian NFTs and BHAKTI loyalty tokens. Guardians propose and vote on the future of the mortal realm.

### RAKSHA Tokens

Soulbound NFTs held by 8 Lokapala guardians. Each RAKSHA grants 1 vote in governance proposals.

### BHAKTI Tokens

Transferable ERC20 tokens representing citizen loyalty and influence. 100M supply distributed between the guardians.

### Governance Process

Proposals require 50% quorum (4 guardians). Voting period lasts ~2 days (43,200 blocks).
